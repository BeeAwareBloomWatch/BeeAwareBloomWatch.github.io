About BeeAware

Our Mission: To provide farmers, beekeepers, and conservationists with accessible, data-driven tools to support pollinator populations. By visualizing near real-time vegetation blooms, we can help ensure the health of bee colonies, which are vital to our food supply.

The Team: Built with love from Clemson during the 2025 NASA Space Apps Challenge.

Data Sources: This project leverages publicly available data from NASA's Earth observation satellites, processed to identify vegetation health and potential floral blooms.
